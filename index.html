The Life of Delsin Rowe

Livin' life as a teen can be very hard, but imagine having superpowers. Life would be so easy... WRONG! Now this is the story of Delsin, a troubled teenager with superpowers, life has never been easy for Delsin. At a young age his parents passed away leaving him with his Grandmother and older Brother. He never asked superpowers, but they were just forced upon him. He is trying to live a regular life while searching for a cure to get rid of his powers. The police hunt down individuals like him as they are considered a threat to society.

Will he find a cure?

Will he use his power for good or bad?

Or will he be captured by the police?
